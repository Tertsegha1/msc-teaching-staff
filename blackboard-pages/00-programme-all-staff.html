Dr. Tertsegha Joseph Anande

Lecturer & Deputy Programme Leader
PhD FHEA MBCS
CMP701
Dr. Tertsegha Joseph Anande is a Lecturer, Machine Learning Researcher, and Cyber Security Specialist at Ulster University QAHE, Birmingham. A Fellow of the Higher Education Academy (FHEA) and Member of the British Computer Society (MBCS), Dr. Anande brings a wealth of academic and applied expertise to the MSc Computer Science and Technology programme. His work spans postgraduate and undergraduate instruction, with a particular focus on equipping students with practical, industry-relevant skills in data science, machine learning, and digital transformation. Dr. Anande is also the host of the widely followed 'Re-Inventing Myself' weekly series, which demystifies Automated Machine Learning (AutoML) for general and professional audiences.
Research & Expertise
Automated Machine Learning (AutoML), Cyber Security, Data Science, Feature Engineering, Model Development and Deployment
